Solutions for Dryland Resilience

At ADNRC, we translate research into field-ready solutions. Working with communities, governments, NGOs, and the private sector, we co-design interventions across water, energy, food, and ecosystems to build resilient dryland livelihoods.

Our Solutions Approach

We blend rigorous science, open data, and participatory design. We prioritize scalable, low-regret interventions that reduce vulnerability while enhancing long-term sustainability.

Key Solution Areas

Water Security

Groundwater monitoring, recharge enhancement, solar-powered water systems, and drought early-warning systems.

Sustainable Food Systems

Climate-smart cropping, agroforestry, restoration-based grazing, and market-based resilience measures.

Energy for Sustainable Water

Solar irrigation, off-grid energy for productive use, and integrated water-energy planning for drylands.

Ecosystem Restoration

Rangeland restoration, native species re-establishment, carbon sequestration initiatives, and nature-based solutions.

Decision Support & Tools

Interactive dashboards, mapping tools, WEFE trade-off models, and localized scenario planning for policymakers and communities.

Capacity & Policy

Training, co-development of policy briefs, and institutional strengthening to embed resilient practices at scale.

Working Together

Effective solutions require partnerships. ADNRC collaborates with local communities, county governments, research institutions, and international agencies to co-design, test, and scale interventions that are equitable and evidence-based.
