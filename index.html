African Drylands Nexus Research Center (ADNRC)

𝗧𝗵𝗲 𝗔𝗳𝗿𝗶𝗰𝗮𝗻 𝗗𝗿𝘆𝗹𝗮𝗻𝗱𝘀 𝗡𝗲𝘅𝘂𝘀 𝗥𝗲𝘀𝗲𝗮𝗿𝗰𝗵 𝗖𝗲𝗻𝘁𝗲𝗿 (𝗔𝗗𝗡𝗥𝗖) is a newly established, Kenya-based, research-driven NGO dedicated to advancing research on climate change and the Water–Energy–Food–Ecosystem (WEFE) nexus in Africa’s drylands.

Our Focus

We bring together researchers, communities, and policymakers to co-create solutions that respond to the climate crisis in Africa’s drylands. ADNRC focuses on applied research, data-driven insights, and policy support for sustainable water, agriculture, and ecosystem management.

Latest Highlights

  • New research initiative on drought resilience in Northern Kenya.
  • Open data platform for groundwater and climate information.
  • Collaborations with regional and international partners to advance dryland sustainability.
