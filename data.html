Welcome to the ADNRC Data Portal

ADNRC provides curated datasets to support science, policy, and development work in Africa's drylands, including climate, water, energy, food, and ecosystem data. Our open data initiative aims to advance research and innovation for sustainable dryland development.

All datasets are carefully quality-controlled, documented, and made available under open licenses to maximize their use and impact.
