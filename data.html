ADNRC Data Portal

The ADNRC Data Portal provides access to datasets, maps, and tools supporting research and decision-making in Africa’s drylands. We curate Water–Energy–Food–Ecosystems (WEFE) datasets with a strong climate lens, prioritizing open, well-documented, and actionable data.

Tools & Platforms

  • Drylands Map Explorer: Interactive maps for water points, vegetation, and climate anomalies.
  • Climate Risk Dashboard: Drought monitoring, seasonal forecasts, and impact outlooks.
  • Groundwater Atlas: Aquifer boundaries, recharge zones, quality classes, and well logs.
  • Food Systems Monitor: Crop conditions, market alerts, and nutrition risk overlays.
  • Energy Planning Toolkit: Solar PV suitability, mini-grid siting, and water-energy modeling.

Access, Licensing & Formats

ADNRC promotes open science. Most datasets are released under Creative Commons licenses. Sensitive data may require a request or partnership agreement. Downloads are typically available as CSV, GeoJSON, and TIFF/NetCDF, with metadata in JSON or ISO-19115.

Contribute & Collaborate

We welcome data contributions from researchers, agencies, and communities. To share a dataset or request curation support, contact our data team via Support.
