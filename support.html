Support ADNRC

Your support strengthens our mission to enhance water, energy, food, and ecosystem resilience across Africa’s drylands. Whether through funding, partnerships, or data sharing, your contribution drives real change in communities facing climate and environmental challenges.

1. Fund Our Research

Financial contributions directly support field research, data platforms, and community-driven projects. By funding ADNRC, you are investing in solutions to drought, food insecurity, and sustainable energy in drylands.

2. Partner With Us

We collaborate with universities, governments, NGOs, and international organizations to deliver high-impact research and solutions. Partnerships enable us to co-design projects and scale innovations for long-term resilience.

3. Share Data and Knowledge

Open data and shared expertise are key to advancing our mission. If you hold relevant datasets, tools, or indigenous knowledge, partnering with ADNRC ensures they contribute to science and practice across drylands.

4. Volunteer and Contribute Skills

Join us as a research intern, field volunteer, or technical advisor. Your time and expertise directly contribute to projects in water management, renewable energy, agriculture, and ecosystem restoration.

Get in Touch

To explore how you can support ADNRC, please email us or reach out via our contact form. Together, we can build a more resilient future for Africa’s drylands.
