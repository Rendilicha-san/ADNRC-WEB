About Us

𝗧𝗵𝗲 𝗔𝗳𝗿𝗶𝗰𝗮𝗻 𝗗𝗿𝘆𝗹𝗮𝗻𝗱𝘀 𝗡𝗲𝘅𝘂𝘀 𝗥𝗲𝘀𝗲𝗮𝗿𝗰𝗵 𝗖𝗲𝗻𝘁𝗲𝗿 (𝗔𝗗𝗡𝗥𝗖) is a Kenya-based, research-driven NGO dedicated to advancing climate change and Water–Energy–Food–Ecosystem (WEFE) nexus research across Africa’s drylands. Building on the same mission highlighted on our home page, ADNRC serves as a regional hub that connects science, policy, and community action. We aim to transform evidence into solutions that secure water, food, and livelihoods while restoring fragile ecosystems in the face of climate extremes.

Our Vision

A resilient and sustainable drylands region where people, ecosystems, and economies coexist in balance, supported by sound science and inclusive decision-making.

Our Mission

To generate and share knowledge, data, and practical solutions that strengthen resilience in Africa’s drylands. We bring together communities, governments, and research partners to address water, food, energy, ecosystem, and climate challenges in an integrated way.

Our Values

  • Excellence: We uphold rigorous science and evidence-based approaches.
  • Collaboration: We co-create solutions with communities, governments, and international partners.
  • Innovation: We apply new tools, technologies, and ideas to old and emerging challenges.
  • Integrity: We are committed to transparency, accountability, and ethical practice.
  • Impact: We ensure our research drives action for people and the environment.

Our Impact

From piloting groundwater management systems in Northern Kenya to developing integrated approaches for climate resilience, ADNRC works at the frontline of science and development. Our research informs policy, guides investments, and supports community-led initiatives that restore ecosystems, secure water and food, and build resilience to climate shocks.
